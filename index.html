About Domestic Angels

Founded in 2015 with a passion for cleaning.

Since 2015 Domestic Angels has grown with a team of professional and friendly domestic cleaners. We serve the North Coast in Northern Ireland and are expanding by the year. We take pride in our friendly customer service and strong rapport with our customers as well as our exceedingly high standards and expectations when carrying out our services.
