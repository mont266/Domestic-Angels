About Domestic Angels

We are a reputable domestic cleaning business operation in the causeway coast of Northern Ireland. We clean personal and commercial premises and have long-standing relationships with a lot of our clients. We are seeing an increase of business and are struggling to keep up with demand and are looking for enthusiastic, driven and committed workers to join our team.

We provide all cleaning supplies, equipment, uniform and training required to complete the job to a satisfactory standard.

You will be given a 10 hour contract, but will be offered overtime most weeks as a lot of our jobs can be sporadic.

Requirements

We pride ourselves in providing a high-level of customer service to our clients. Below are some of the requirements for this job role.

  • Must be over the age of 18.

  • Must hold a full UK Drivers License and have their own car.

  • Experience in domestic cleaning isn't necessary but would be a massive bonus.

  • Must be willing to put in atleast 10 hours of work per week.

  • Must be willing to provide a DBS criminal record check.

  • Must be comfortable entering client's homes.

If you match the above requirements and you think you'd be a great fit for joining our team, we'd love to hear from you!
