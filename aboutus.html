About Us

Anne Montford founded Domestic Angels in 2015. The foundations of the company orignally started as a side project for Anne, alongside her full time job. Since 2015 Domestic Angels has grown with a team of professional and friendly domestic cleaners. We serve the North Coast in Northern Ireland and are expanding by the year.
We take pride in our friendly customer service and strong rapport with our customers as well as our exceedingly high standards and expectations when carrying out our services.
There truly is no job too big or too small for our team and we will always take on a challenging job where others won't.
